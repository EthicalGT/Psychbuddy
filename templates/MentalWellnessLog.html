Psychbuddy Mental Wellness Log is a dedicated feature designed to support patients in documenting their daily experiences and emotions. This tool allows users to write about their day, including thoughts, feelings, and incidents, creating a personal space for reflection. By consistently logging entries, users can track their mental and emotional progress over time.

For psychiatrists, this journal provides valuable insights into the patient's mindset and behavioral patterns, enabling them to better understand their patient's mental state. This feature serves as a collaborative tool between patient and psychiatrist, fostering deeper communication, personalized care, and more effective treatment plans.
